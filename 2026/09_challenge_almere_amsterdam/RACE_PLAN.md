## Pre-Race Checklist (lessons from Bonn 2026)

Each line is a concrete fix for something that went wrong at Bonn 2026.

**Night before**
- [ ] **Test the full kit** and locate TriSuit pockets; decide which pocket holds which gel.
- [ ] **Elastic laces fitted and rehearsed** — quick on/off, no bending in T2.
- [ ] **Nutrition is tested product only** — nothing new.
- [ ] **Apply vaseline plan** — back of the neck to prevent wetsuit chafing (Bonn lesson).
- [ ] Bike serviced/waxed, tyres checked.

**Race morning (at check-in)**
- [ ] **Calibrate the power meter** before racking.
- [ ] **Lay out socks where they can be found** — not stuffed in the shoes (Bonn T1 lesson).
- [ ] **Mount bottles**: at least one plain water plus carb mix.
- [ ] Pump tyres to race pressure.
- [ ] **Memorize the rack path** for both T1 and T2.
- [ ] **Apply sunscreen** and pack a **running cap** (buy one — Bonn lesson).
- [ ] Two swim caps with goggle straps tucked under the outer one.

**Skills to practice before race day (Bonn carry-overs)**
- [ ] Downhill / descending confidence (less relevant here — flat course).
- [ ] Removing cycling shoes in the dismount zone heading to T2.
- [ ] Upper-body strengthening for the swim (shoulders/chest/biceps).
